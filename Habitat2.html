Grassland Habitat

The Grassland habitat at Moon night zoo is spacious and open with lots of grass and rocks to try and mimic the real grassland habitats in which our beautiful animals came from

Grace the giraffe

The giraffe, nature's enchanting skyscraper, is a graceful marvel of the animal kingdom. With its elegantly elongated neck, distinctive patchwork coat of irregular spots, and long, sinuous legs, the giraffe embodies both majesty and uniqueness.

Jake the Hartebeest

Jake the Hartebeest is a striking and distinctive member of our zoo's diverse animal family. With his elegant and robust build, Jake embodies the essence of the African grasslands he hails from. His rust-colored coat is accentuated by contrasting white facial markings, which add to his captivating appearance. Jake is known for his remarkable agility, often seen sprinting gracefully across his enclosure, showcasing the Hartebeest's incredible speed and endurance.
