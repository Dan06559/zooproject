Freshwater Habitat

Welcome to our Freshwater Oasis, a serene and captivating haven that transports you into the mesmerizing world of freshwater ecosystems. This enchanting habitat showcases the diverse aquatic life found in rivers, streams, ponds, and wetlands from around the globe, all within the immersive setting of our zoo.

Alex the alligator

Welcome to our Freshwater Exhibit, where you'll encounter Alex, the undoubted monarch of this aquatic domain. Alex the Alligator is a true embodiment of primordial strength and the captivating allure of North America's remarkable reptiles. With a habitat that mirrors the lush, thriving wetlands, our freshwater exhibit provides a perfect stage for this awe-inspiring creature.

Al the electric eel

Prepare to be astounded by Al, our extraordinary Electric Eel, in a dedicated aquatic exhibit that showcases the incredible capabilities of this aquatic marvel. Al's unique and captivating presence provides an electrifying experience that leaves visitors in awe of the wonders of the underwater world.
