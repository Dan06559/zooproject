Desert Habitat

The Desert Oasis is a carefully crafted environment that replicates the rugged charm of desert landscapes. Featuring sandy terrain, rocky outcrops, and arid vegetation, the exhibit immerses visitors in the unique atmosphere of deserts.

Zack the Tortoise

Zack the Tortoise, a beloved resident of our zoo, is a magnificent example of the enduring and majestic desert tortoise species. With his sturdy, domed shell and plodding gait, Zack embodies the timeless charm of these gentle giants that have roamed the arid landscapes of the desert for centuries. His intricately patterned carapace, colored in various shades of brown and beige, serves as a testament to the rugged beauty of the desert.

Lewis the cheetah

Meet Lewis, the epitome of grace and speed in our zoo's animal kingdom. Lewis is a cheetah, the fastest land animal on the planet, and he captures the hearts of visitors with his striking appearance and mesmerizing presence. His sleek, golden coat, adorned with beautiful black spots, is a testament to the natural artistry of the animal world
