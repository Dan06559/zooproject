Mountain Habitat

The Mountain Habitat at Moon night zoo is meticulously designed to replicate the multiple ecosystems found in high-altitude regions across the globe. From the rugged Rockies to the towering Himalayas. This amazingly designed Mountain habitat is also made to make our amazing animal friends feel like they are home

Here at moon night zoo we have a mountain leopard named george coming to this beatiful mountain habitat to showcase his beautiful spotted coat along with his great strength and huge size.

Shadow the Cougar

Cougars are solitary and highly adaptable big cats that inhabit a diverse range of ecosystems, from dense forests to arid deserts. They are known for their tawny fur, powerful physique, and keen predatory instincts. Adult cougars typically weigh between 90 to 200 pounds and measure 6 to 9 feet in length, including their long tails. These cats are known for their incredible agility and can jump up to 18 feet vertically and cover ground swiftly, making them formidable hunters.
