Rainforest Habitat

Welcome to our Rainforest Habitat at Moon night Zoo. Step into the heart of the world's most enchanting and biodiverse ecosystems, where adventure and discovery await around every lush corner. Our Rainforest Habitat is a remarkable haven that transports you to the depths of this tropical wonderland without leaving the city.

Josh the Jaguar

Prepare to be mesmerized as you encounter Josh, our resident jaguar, in the heart of our zoo's lush and enchanting rainforest habitat. Josh is the embodiment of stealth, power, and untamed beauty, representing the apex of the New World's big cats. His sleek, golden coat is adorned with mesmerizing rosette patterns that glisten like works of art in the dappled sunlight.

Sylvia the sloth

Meet Sylvia, our delightful resident sloth, who embodies the essence of tranquility within the bustling world of our zoo. Sylvia is a marvel of nature, showcasing the art of unhurried living with her languid movements and endearing smile. Her soft, dense fur, a mosaic of earthy hues, adds to her charm as she leisurely navigates her lush, tree-filled enclosure.
